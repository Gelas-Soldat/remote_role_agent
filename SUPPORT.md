# Support Remote Role Agent

If this project helps you find better roles, organize your job search, or build your own local job agent, there are a few simple ways to support it.

1. Star the repository on GitHub.
2. Share practical feedback through Issues.
3. Suggest useful job sources or safer location filters.
4. Support future development through the Sponsor button, Buy Me a Coffee, or thanks.dev.
